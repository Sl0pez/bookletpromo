August's Story

August is a Senior at Manhattan's East Side Community School. She lives in Astoria, Queens and braves a quick 30 minute commute to and from school everyday that can last as long as 3 hours.

This choose your own adventure style zine features the different delays and adventures that August has during her commute.

about the author

I'm Santiago Lopez. I'm currently a sophomore at the New School majoring in Journalism and Design. The inspiration for this zine comes from my frustration with commute and the realization that there's always someone out there with a commute that's much worse than mine.
